## 1. Organisation Context

### Internal Context
- Fintech organisation processing digital payments
- 100 employees across Bengaluru HQ and Mumbai office
- Core systems hosted on AWS cloud infrastructure
- Internal development team building payment APIs

### External Context
- Regulated by Reserve Bank of India (RBI)
- Subject to India's Personal Data Protection Bill (PDPB)
- Processes sensitive customer financial data
- Dependent on third-party payment gateways

---

## 2. Interested Parties

| Stakeholder | Requirements |
|-------------|-------------|
| RBI | Compliance with cybersecurity framework |
| Customers | Protection of personal and financial data |
| Employees | Clear security policies and training |
| Suppliers | Defined security requirements in contracts |
| Auditors | Evidence of ISMS implementation |
| Board | Risk visibility and compliance status |

---

## 3. ISMS Scope

**In Scope:**
- All information assets supporting payment processing
- AWS cloud infrastructure — EC2, S3, RDS, IAM
- Corporate network — Bengaluru HQ and Mumbai office
- All employees and contractors accessing company systems
- Third-party suppliers with access to company data

**Out of Scope:**
- Personal devices not connected to corporate network
- Subsidiary companies with separate IT infrastructure

---

## 4. Scope Statement

The ISMS applies to the design, development, operation, and 
support of payment processing services at ABC India Pvt. Ltd., 
including all information assets, personnel, processes, and 
technology within the Bengaluru and Mumbai locations and 
associated AWS cloud infrastructure.
