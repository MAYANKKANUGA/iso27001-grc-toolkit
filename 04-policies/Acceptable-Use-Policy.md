# Acceptable Use Policy

**Organisation:** ABC India Pvt. Ltd.
**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** March 2025
**Author:** Mayank Kanuga
**Standard:** ISO/IEC 27001:2022 — A.5.10

---

## 1. Purpose
Define acceptable use of ABC India Pvt. Ltd. information 
assets, systems, and network resources.

---

## 2. Acceptable Use

### 2.1 Permitted Activities
- Business-related work and communication
- Accessing approved cloud services and applications
- Using company devices for authorised work purposes

### 2.2 Prohibited Activities
- Accessing, downloading, or sharing illegal content
- Installing unauthorised software on company devices
- Sharing login credentials with other users
- Connecting personal devices to corporate network without approval
- Accessing systems outside of assigned role permissions
- Using company resources for personal business or profit

---

## 3. Internet and Email Use

- Internet access is monitored and filtered
- Business email must not be used for personal purposes
- Suspicious emails must be reported to IT Security immediately
- Email attachments from unknown senders must not be opened

---

## 4. Data Handling

- Customer data must never be stored on personal devices
- Sensitive data must be encrypted before transmission
- Data must not be uploaded to unapproved cloud services
- USB drives are prohibited unless approved by IT Manager

---

## 5. Monitoring

Users are informed that company systems are monitored. 
Use of company systems implies consent to monitoring 
in accordance with applicable laws.

---

## 6. Violations

Violations will result in disciplinary action up to and 
including termination. Criminal violations will be 
reported to law enforcement.
