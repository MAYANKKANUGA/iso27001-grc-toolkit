# Information Security Policy

**Organisation:** ABC India Pvt. Ltd.
**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** March 2025
**Author:** Mayank Kanuga
**Approved By:** CEO
**Standard:** ISO/IEC 27001:2022 — Clause 5.2

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the information security direction and 
principles for ABC India Pvt. Ltd. to protect the confidentiality, 
integrity, and availability of all information assets.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, consultants, 
and third parties who access ABC India Pvt. Ltd. information 
assets, systems, and facilities.

---

## 3. Information Security Objectives

- Protect customer financial data from unauthorised access
- Comply with RBI cybersecurity guidelines and PDPB
- Ensure 99.9% availability of payment processing systems
- Respond to security incidents within 4 hours
- Conduct annual security awareness training for all staff
- Achieve ISO 27001:2022 certification within 12 months

---

## 4. Policy Statements

### 4.1 Confidentiality
All information assets must be classified and handled according 
to the information classification scheme. Customer data must 
never be shared with unauthorised parties.

### 4.2 Integrity
All information must be accurate, complete, and protected from 
unauthorised modification. Changes to critical systems must 
follow the change management procedure.

### 4.3 Availability
Critical systems must be available as per defined SLAs. 
Business continuity plans must be maintained and tested annually.

### 4.4 Access Control
Access to information assets must be granted on a need-to-know 
and least privilege basis. All access must be reviewed quarterly.

### 4.5 Incident Management
All security incidents must be reported to the IT Security team 
immediately. Incidents must be documented and lessons learned 
recorded.

### 4.6 Compliance
All staff must comply with applicable laws, regulations, and 
contractual obligations including RBI guidelines, PDPB, and 
ISO 27001:2022.

### 4.7 Risk Management
Information security risks must be assessed annually and after 
significant changes. Risk treatment decisions must be approved 
by senior management.

---

## 5. Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| CEO | Approve and endorse this policy |
| CISO | Implement and maintain ISMS |
| IT Manager | Implement technical controls |
| HR Manager | Ensure staff compliance and training |
| All Staff | Comply with this policy and report incidents |

---

## 6. Policy Violations

Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action 
including termination of employment and legal action where 
applicable.

---

## 7. Review

This policy will be reviewed annually or after significant 
changes to the organisation or threat landscape.
